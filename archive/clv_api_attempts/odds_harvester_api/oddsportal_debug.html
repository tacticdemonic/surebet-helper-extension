NBA Betting Odds & Markets

Supplying players with odds comparisons from the best online bookmakers ensure that players always have the best NBA odds for today when betting on NBA outrights or games.

The NBA outright market consists of who will win the NBA Championship, while the NBA matches have a larger list of markets to choose from, including the most common markets and some more obscure markets too.

NBA betting odds can be found for popular markets like the moneyline and spreads markets, as well as quarter and half betting, half-time/full-time, and more.

Our rapidly changing, dynamic odds comparisons will be available for the below markets, while you can claim sports betting offers with those suggested online betting sites providing the best NBA odds.

Moneyline

The moneyline market is where you bet on the outcome of the match, choosing either the home team or away team to win, including overtime (OT).

Over/Under

You can find lots of different options for the over/under market starting sometimes as low as over or under 180.5 points, up to 240.5 points for NBA lines, and maybe more for full-time (inc. OT). This also includes quarters and halves too.

Spread

NBA spreads enables you to bet on a team to cover a line, say -6.5 which means they need to win by 7 points or more. Similarly, you can bet on +6.5 and that means the team you have selected must lose by 6 points or less, while they can also win the game.

NBA 2025/2026 Games Schedule

NBA games can take place on any day throughout the week, and teams can often play on consecutive days due to the hectic schedule. Overall, each team plays 82 NBA games in the regular season and they begin in October and end in April.

Teams who make it to the NBA playoffs will play even more games, and any teams involved in the play-in that make it to the NBA Finals could play an extraordinary amount of games.

In the playoffs, each matchup will be decided by a seven game series as teams progress and attempt to make it to the NBA Finals and potentially win the Championship.

Through our leading online betting sites, we provide competitive NBA odds from the very first game through to the NBA Finals.

In addition to the NBA regular season and NBA playoffs, there is also the NBA in-season tournament, which was introduced in 2023 and something we also have odds comparisons available for.

NBA Standings: Eastern & Western Conferences

There are two different conferences that determine the NBA standings, the Eastern Conference and Western Conference. This is so that teams based in Western USA will regularly play games against each other, and the teams in the East do the same.

Additionally, you will also be able to find the Atlantic Division, Southeast Division, Central Division, Northwest Division, Pacific Division, and Southwest Division on our NBA standings page too.

The NBA standings determine who makes it into the playoffs, who will participate in the play-in to make it into the playoffs, and finally who will be eliminated with no chance of winning the NBA Championship.

Once the season reaches the playoffs stage, a bracket will be visible where you will consistently be able to see the progress for each matchup right to the end of the season and the NBA Finals.

The NBA standings, including the regular season and playoffs, can be seen for this season but also for all seasons dating back to 1998/99.

Through the NBA standings page, you can also see form for up to 30 games, and specifically home or away form to decipher who performs better in front of their own fans or on the road.
